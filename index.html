About me

My name is Jairzinho Adeyina and I am a Front-End developer based in Nara, Japan. I currently work as an English teacher.

In a previous job, I had the opportunity to work on a project to translate our company website to Japanese.The project exposed me to web development and I was immediately enamored by the process. After that I started studying web development in my free time and eventually joined a boot camp.

I enjoy coding pixel perfect and responsive web pages. When I am not coding, I like being active, baking treats with my wife and discovering new places in Western Japan.
